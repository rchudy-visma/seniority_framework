The Promotion Process

This is the official process for requesting a promotion. The process is initiated by you in discussion with your manager.

For full details, see the official Promotion Process on Space.

1

Discussion with Manager

You initiate a discussion about promotion with your direct manager. If you both agree, the manager contacts HR to start the process.

2

Mandatory Technical Interview

HR arranges a technical interview with an experienced interviewer to ensure your seniority is aligned with Visma-wide standards. This step is required for all promotions.

✓

Positive Outcome

Positive feedback from the interview is a prerequisite for promotion. The final decision, however, rests with your manager. If they approve the promotion, they will discuss the details (including salary) with HR, who will finalize the changes.

!

Negative Outcome

If the interview indicates that you do not yet meet the requirements, you and your manager will set clear development goals for the next period (typically 1 year).

Frequently Asked Questions

When can I ask for a promotion?

Typically, the earliest is after one year at Visma, unless a different timeline was agreed upon at hiring.

Do I have to do a technical interview?

Yes, it is a mandatory step to ensure consistent seniority levels across the company.

What if I disagree with my current seniority level?

You can request a technical interview via HR at any time to get an objective assessment, even outside of a promotion request.
